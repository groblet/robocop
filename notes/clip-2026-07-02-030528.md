# Second Brain System: Executive Summary

**BLUF:** To build a functional second brain, capture everything immediately into a single, searchable digital inbox and review it weekly to prevent clutter.

### ⚡ Core Objective
* Create an external system to offload mental strain.
* Ensure your mind is free to process information rather than store it.

### 📥 Step 1: Central Capture
* Use one primary app (such as Notion, Apple Notes, or Obsidian) to dump all ideas, tasks, and bookmarks.
* Treat this as a temporary holding zone. Do not try to organise notes at the moment of capture.

### 🧹 Step 2: The Weekly Review
* Dedicate a specific time each week to clear out your inbox.
* Archive completed items, categorise active projects, and delete what is no longer useful.

### 🧠 ADHD Optimization
* Keep the system low-friction. If taking a note takes more than two clicks, the system will fail.
* Rely entirely on global search functions instead of creating deep, complex folder structures.
